Gourd pier cultural center grab wedding song and dance to reproduce the original culture and customs

  • Release date：2015/09/15
  • Release unit：臺中市立葫蘆墩文化中心
  • Times watched：204

The “2017 Better Together, a Wonderful Night for All” Double Ten Day celebration is leaving Taipei for the first time this year, and will instead be held in Taichung. The festivities will begin at 7 p.m. on October 9 at the track of the National Taiwan University of Physical Education and Sport. Arts heavyweights including Chio Tian Folk Drums and Arts Troupe, Nine One One, GTM, William Wei, Eve Ai, Bii, Elvis Tian, and Pets Tseng for a night of revelry! Members of the public may pick up free tickets from local Taichung City Government offices beginning today.

On September 28, the Cultural Affairs Bureau of the Taichung City Government held a press conference to announce the hosting of the Double Ten celebration at the City Hall on Taiwan Boulevard. The Chio Tian Folk Drums and Arts Troupe and boy band GTM gave a rousing performance at the conference after the announcement. Chief of the Legislative Yuan and Chair of the National Day Planning Committee Su Jiaquan, Overseas Community Affairs Council Head Wu Xinxing, Vice Chief of the Ministry of the Interior Hua Jingqun, City Mayor Lin Jialong, Deputy Mayor Lin Lingsan, City Council Member Liu Shizhou, Head of the World Taiwanese Chambers of Commerce Lin Guixiang, and Event Ambassadors Nine One One were all in attendance.

Yuan Chief Su stated that last year’s “One Heart across China” Double Ten Day celebration was held specially for overseas Chinese and Taiwanese businesspeople, and was held in Taipei. This year, the event is moving from Taipei, and, after considering that Taichung City residents would happily take part in the music, cultural, and arts events, the committee decided to “give the event to Mayor Lin and we won’t be disappointed!” and so decided to hold this year’s Double Ten Day celebration in Taichung. We hope that all citizens of Taiwan may also attend this event, show our love for Taiwan, and strive for the future of Taiwan.
